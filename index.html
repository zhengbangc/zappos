A Little About Me

“People can be really smart or have skills that are directly applicable, but if they don’t really believe in it, then they are not going to really work hard”

Zhengbang Chen

Developer & Design Thinker

My name is Zhengbang Chen, currently a junior Computer Engineering Student at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My main interests are Software Development, Front-end development and product management.


I am looking for experience and opportunities as a Mobile Front End Development Intern for Zappos, Summer 2016. I am really looking forward to learn the design thinking and software-developing philosophy as a Zappos Family member, since becoming a full-stack developer is my goal. Also, I am expecting to be part of an entity to offer incredible user experience through design and development. So if you are looking for a full-stack-to-be programmer into your team, don't hesitate to consider me! My Resume Download Here
